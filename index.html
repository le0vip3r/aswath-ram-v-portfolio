01 — About

The Story So Far

From circuit boards to capture-the-flag — the journey of building an offensive security mindset.

I'm Aswath Ram V, a 3rd-year Electronics and Communication Engineering student at Kumaraguru College of Technology, Coimbatore. My path into cybersecurity started with embedded systems — understanding hardware at the lowest level gave me an instinct for how things break.

I now channel that mindset into offensive security: finding vulnerabilities, writing exploits, and competing in CTF competitions. I believe the best defenders are people who have learned to think like attackers.

My recent highlights include ranking 38th globally in the Alice GenAI Security CTF (all flags captured) and finishing 55th out of 250+ teams at DEFCON DCG91422 2026, organized by DCG Coimbatore.

Outside of security, I play guitar, solve Rubik's cubes, and occasionally write about what I learn on Medium.
